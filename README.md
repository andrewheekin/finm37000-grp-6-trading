# finm37000-grp-6-trading


## Overview
FINM37000 Summer '26 Group 6 will be creating a trading strategy for Interest Rate derivatives (and possibly a hybrid-domain strategy including Energy derivatives, given 2 group members ranked Energy as their top market interest).

## Trading Strategy Decisions
Group 6 will need to pin down the following about our trading strategy:

**Scope & Thesis*
1. **Products**: What products do we want to trade? Treasury futures, SOFR, Crude, etc?
2. **Type of Strategy**: Macro-driven, carry, etc?
3. **Holding Period**: Will we trade HFT (sub-second), intraday, multi-day, longer?
4. **Thesis**: What is our group's trading thesis around interest rates?
